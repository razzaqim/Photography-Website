About me

I am a professional photographer providing photoshoots for babies. I am Birmingham based and will travel to your home so your baby is comfortable with the environment the shoot is taking place in. I have over 10 years of experience and will surely capture those speical moments with your babies! I specialise in photography for newborns up until the first year of their lives.
